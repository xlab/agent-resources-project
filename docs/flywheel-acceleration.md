## Creator Activation

### 6. Recruit 10 Creator Champions

Each creator brings their network. 10 active creators = 10x content + 10 distribution channels.

**Playbook**:
1. Identify Claude Code power users (Discord, Twitter, GitHub)
2. Personal outreach: "Would you create a skill? I'll feature you prominently"
3. Help them through their first skill
4. Celebrate and amplify their launch

**Leverage**: Creators become evangelists. Their networks become your reach.

**Format**:

**Leverage**: Creates ongoing content + incentivizes quality.
